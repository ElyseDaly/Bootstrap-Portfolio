About me

Elyse Daly is a Research Assistant for Dr. Bonnie Spring's lab in Northwestern University's Department of Preventive Medicine in Chicago, IL. The Spring Lab conducts research to test approaches to weight loss and smoking cessation using wearable devices and apps. Elyse's goal is to be able to create her own apps and websites to conduct health and wellness research, using her newfound web development knowledge.

Elyse has lived in Chicago for the last 3 years. When she is not seeing research participants in lab or coding like crazy, you'll probably find her playing videogames, playing soccer, watching terrible reality television, or reading not so terrible books.
